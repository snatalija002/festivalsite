Watch it Outside

This year's festival ‘Watch it outside Boston edition’ is organized by Clara Dunn's Watch it Outside events Co. in Boston. The films will be screened from August 5th to 8th in Boston Public Garden, a park in Boston that can hold up to 1,500 people.


People of all ages are welcome and shows will be free and open to everyone, but if you want to come you need to pre-register. You can do that by clicking on the register button on the top right corner!


You can watch some of the best movies of all time from comedies like "Blockers" and horrors like "Sinister" to some of Al Pacino's best works like "Scarface" every day from 6pm till midnight!


We can't wait to help the public discover awesome films!
